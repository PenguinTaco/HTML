Animal Crossing is a video game for many systems in which you play as a person that moves to a small remote village populated with anthropomorphic animals.

This game series has a larger female demographic than a male demograpgic and usually has people buying new systems just to play the game.

These games are usually among there respective consoles best selling games. Animal Crossing sold 2.32 million copies; Animal Crossing Wild World sold 11.75 Million copies; Animal Crossing City Folk sold 3.38 Million copies; and Animal Crossing New Leaf sold 11.59 Million copies.

Reviews for these games are usually good
